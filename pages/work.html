Work by renewal


We remake old unique clothes so that they are not thrown away and can be worn for a longer period of time. This way we don't waste clothes and we make the world more sustainable.

The service we have is from high-quality end. We do the best we can to make our customers happy with their new clothes.

Our team


Our team includes 27 hardworking employees. We all do our best to provide the best service to the customer.



As an innovation, we ensure that our team does not feel like a work team, but rather like a family. We treat everyone equally and every team member is important to us.
