Renewal


The United Repair Centre is the result of an alliance between the Amsterdam Economic Board, Makers Unite, a social venture, and outdoor apparel company Patagonia. We are proponents of inclusivity and circularity. In all that we do.

When we combine the two, we can build a world in which everything and everyone may prosper and live in harmony with one another.

Our team
